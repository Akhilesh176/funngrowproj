Questions

Brand FAQs

How quickly can we launch a campaign?
+
Starter campaigns go live in 72 hours, Growth campaigns in 48 hours, and Enterprise campaigns in 24 hours. Our team reviews your brief, matches it to the right teen segments, and activates the campaign.
How are teens verified? Is the data accurate?
+
All teens undergo phone number verification and profile screening. Our AI fraud detection flags anomalies in real-time. Survey responses have a 94% accuracy rate, validated against behavioural consistency checks.
What kind of ROI can we expect?
+
Referral campaigns average 4.2× ROI versus Meta/Google ads for the same audience. Survey costs run 60-70% lower than traditional research panels. UGC campaigns generate content at 80% lower cost than agency-produced content.
Is Funngro DPDP / privacy compliant?
+
Yes. We are fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023. All data collection follows informed consent protocols. Brands receive aggregated, anonymised data only — never raw PII.
Can we target specific cities, age groups or interests?
+
Yes. Growth and Enterprise plans offer full audience targeting: city (40+ cities), age (13–24), gender, device type, interests (fashion, gaming, food, etc.), and spending behaviour. Starter plans include 2 targeting filters.
What happens if campaign performance is below expectations?
+
We offer a 7-day performance guarantee. If your campaign doesn't hit the agreed engagement targets in the first 7 days, we extend the campaign at no extra cost until the targets are met.
