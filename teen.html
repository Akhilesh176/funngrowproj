Frequently Asked
Questions

Who can join Funngro?
+
Any Indian student aged 13–24 can join Funngro. You just need a smartphone, a valid phone number and a UPI ID or bank account for withdrawals.
Is Funngro completely free?
+
Yes! Funngro is 100% free to download and use. We never charge teens any fees. Brands pay us; teens earn from us.
How much can I realistically earn?
+
Earnings vary by effort and location. Most active teens earn ₹1,000–₹5,000 per month. Top earners make ₹10,000+ monthly through content creation and high-value campaigns.
How fast are withdrawals?
+
UPI withdrawals are processed within 5 minutes. Bank transfers take 1–2 business days. Minimum withdrawal is ₹50.
Is my personal data safe?
+
Absolutely. We are ISO 27001 certified and comply with India's DPDP Act 2023. Your data is never sold. Survey responses are anonymous and aggregated.
Do I need any skills or experience?
+
No experience needed for most tasks. For content creation tasks, basic smartphone skills are enough. Funngro has tasks for all skill levels, from simple surveys to creative campaigns.
